fun facts about cats!

Cats are fascinating animals that have been companions to humans for thousands of years. They can jump up to six times their body length and have excellent balance thanks to their flexible bodies and tails. Cats also communicate in many ways, including purring, meowing, and using different body positions. A group of kittens is called a kindle, and a group of adult cats is called a clowder. With their playful personalities and amazing abilities, cats are some of the most popular pets in the world
